## The Strategy (The Solution & Vision)

I developed IstiGrow with a core focus on Habit Formation and Long-term Retention. The name itself—a blend of Istiqomah (Consistency) and Grow—reflects the two-tier product strategy:

Phase 1: Istiqomah (The Foundation) Focuses on a Frictionless MVP. By providing a minimalist Dzikr counter, the goal is to lower the barrier to entry for users trying to maintain their spiritual habits post-Ramadan.

Phase 2: Grow (The Evolution) Moving beyond simple counting, the "Grow" aspect focuses on Visualizing Progress. My roadmap includes:

Habit Tracking & Streaks: Implementing a streak system to encourage daily consistency.
Data Insights: A personal dashboard showing growth trends over weeks or months, turning abstract spiritual effort into tangible data.
Milestone Achievements: Gamifying the experience with tiered rewards as users reach new levels of discipline.
Product Positioning: IstiGrow is positioned as a "Digital Companion." Unlike heavy apps filled with ads, it prioritizes a clean User Experience (UX) to ensure the user stays focused on their personal growth journey.
